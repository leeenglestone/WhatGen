Alpha

Born 2013-2025

They are growing up in a highly connected world and are often characterized as technologically proficient from an early age.

Gen Z

Born 1995-2012

They are often considered digital natives, having grown up with widespread internet access and mobile devices.

Millenials / Gen Y

Born 1980-1994

They witnessed the rise of the internet and digital technologies during their formative years.

Gen X

Born 1965-1979

The term "Generation X" was popularized by a novel by Douglas Coupland and is associated with a sense of independence and skepticism.

Boomers

Born 1946-1964

It received its name due to the significant increase in birth rates during this period.

Silent

Born 1925-1945

They are called the "Silent Generation" because they came of age during a period of societal conformity and conservatism.
